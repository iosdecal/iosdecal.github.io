Description
The iOS DeCal was started by Professor Dan Garcia (now our Faculty Advisor) in 2001 as MS-DOS X, a student-run Objective-C course for OS X (and later iOS) development. Rebooted in the Fall 2015 semester, the renamed iOS DeCal now operates with the latest devices (e.g. iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Apple TV). By the end of the semester, students will have created a variety of apps (e.g. Utility, Game, Social Network) from scratch, including one entirely of their own design.
