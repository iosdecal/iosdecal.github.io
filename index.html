Overview
This semester, the iOS DeCal is undergoing many exciting changes! For starters, the course will now be taught entirely in Swift. In fact, we will be working on the cutting edge, with Swift 2.0 and Xcode 7. As a result, we're restructuring the class, with fun new homeworks and projects. Our goal is to have students complete the course with a developed app of their own, confident in their ability to program for iOS.
