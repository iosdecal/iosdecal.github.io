Description
This DeCal was started by Professor Dan Garcia (now our Faculty Advisor) in the early 2000s as MS-DOS X, a student-run Objective-C course for OS X (and later iOS) development. Rebooted in the Fall 2015 semester, the renamed iOS DeCal now operates on the cutting edge of the Apple development world, working with the latest tools (e.g. Swift 2, Xcode 7) for the latest devices (e.g. iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Apple TV). The class was restructured with fun new homeworks and projects aimed at ensuring students gain confidence in their ability to program for iOS. By the end of the semester, students will have created a variety of apps (e.g. Utility, Game, Social Network) from scratch, including one entirely of their own design.
